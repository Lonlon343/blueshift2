# DECISIONS.md

## Design Aesthetic
- **Style**: Corporate Glassmorphism ("Corporate and Glass").
- **Vibe**: Professional, transparent, high-end.
- **Hero**: Clean typography with micro-animations. No heavy 3D background in Hero, keep it text-focused.
- **Color Palette**: Deep corporate blues/slate with glass effects (transparency, blur).
