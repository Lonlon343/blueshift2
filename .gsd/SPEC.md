# SPEC.md — Project Specification

> **Status**: `FINALIZED`

## Vision
A performance-rich, premium digital presence for **BlueShift Consulting** that targets small to mid-sized companies. The design must be modern, dynamic, and professional, communicating expertise in digital efficiency, growth, and clear decision-making.

## Goals
1.  **Brand Representation**: Translate the "BlueShift" identity into a high-end visual language (premium typography, fluid animations, professional color palette).
2.  **Service Showcase**: Clearly articulate services (Digital Analysis, Roadmap Development, AI & Automation).
3.  **High Performance**: Achieve top-tier Lighthouse scores (90+) for Performance, SEO, and Accessibility.
4.  **Lead Generation**: drive inquiries through clear calls-to-action (CTA) and contact methods.

## Non-Goals (Out of Scope)
-   Complex backend systems (CMS) - content will be static for v1 unless specified otherwise.
-   User login/authentication.
-   E-commerce functionality.

## Users
-   **Primary**: Owners and decision-makers of small to mid-sized companies (SMBs/SMEs).
-   **Needs**: Trust, clarity, expertise, modernization, efficiency.

## Design Aesthetics
-   **Style**: Premium, minimalist but rich (glassmorphism, subtle gradients, smooth motion).
-   **Vibe**: "Consultancy of the future" — not stuffy/corporate, but sleek and tech-forward.
-   **Interactivity**: Hover effects, scroll reveals (scroll-driven animations), smooth transitions.

## Success Criteria
- [ ] Website loads in under 1.5s on 4G networks.
- [ ] 95+ Lighthouse Performance score.
- [ ] Responsive design works flawlessly on Mobile, Tablet, and Desktop.
- [ ] Contact mechanism is functional and accessible.
